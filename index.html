A Rare Combination of Medicine and Tradition

Welcome. I am a Royal College certified emergency physician and an ordained, certified mohel through the Conservative Movement — a combination that is exceptionally rare, and one I believe makes a meaningful difference for the families I serve. When you invite me into one of the most significant moments of your family's life, you deserve someone who brings both deep medical training and genuine religious knowledge to the experience.

I completed my medical degree at McMaster University and my five-year residency in emergency medicine at the University of Ottawa. I have received formal training in neonatal circumcision, and my expertise in this area has been recognized by the College of Physicians and Surgeons of Ontario. At the same time, I completed the BRIT KODESH course and was ordained and certified through the Rabbinical Assembly of the Conservative Movement to perform ritual circumcision in accordance with Conservative halacha.

My approach is simple: every family deserves a bris milah that is medically safe, ritually meaningful, and conducted with warmth and respect. Whether you are deeply observant or approaching this for the first time, I am here to guide you through every step of the process with honesty, care, and expertise.
