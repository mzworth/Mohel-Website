Welcome

A Combination of Medicine and Tradition

My name is Dr. Max Zworth — I'm a Royal College certified Emergency Physician and an ordained, certified mohel through the Conservative Movement. A bris milah is one of the most significant moments in a family's life, and you deserve someone who takes both dimensions of that moment — the medical and the spiritual — with equal seriousness.

I grew up in Toronto's Jewish community, attending Jewish day school and being involved with Beth Tzedec, a Conservative synagogue. I have always found deep meaning in Jewish ritual and prayer. Going on to train as an Emergency Physician, I saw an opportunity to bring something unique to the community I love: the procedural precision and clinical judgment of an Emergency Physician, combined with genuine knowledge of and commitment to Conservative halacha.

Whether this is your first child or your fourth, whether you are deeply observant or approaching the bris for the first time, I will guide your family through the experience with warmth, honesty, and care. My goal is simple: that the day feels safe and meaningful.
