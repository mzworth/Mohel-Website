Welcome

A Combination of Medicine and Tradition

My name is Dr. Max Zworth — I'm a Royal College certified emergency physician and an ordained, certified mohel through the Conservative Movement. It's a combination I arrived at deliberately, and one I believe makes a real difference for the families I serve. A brit milah is one of the most significant moments in a family's life, and you deserve someone who takes both dimensions of that moment — the medical and the spiritual — with equal seriousness.

I grew up in the Conservative Movement and have always found deep meaning in Jewish ritual and prayer. After completing the Nativ College Leadership Program in Israel and going on to train as an emergency physician at McMaster University and the University of Ottawa, I saw an opportunity to bring something unique to the Jewish community I love: the procedural precision and clinical judgment of a Royal College emergency physician, combined with genuine knowledge of and commitment to Conservative halacha. Becoming a certified mohel wasn't a career move — it was a calling.

Whether this is your first child or your fourth, whether you are deeply observant or thinking about brit milah for the first time, I will guide your family through the experience with warmth, honesty, and care. My goal is simple: that the day feels safe, meaningful, and exactly as it should be.
