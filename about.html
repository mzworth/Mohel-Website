Biography

Dr. Max Zworth is a Royal College certified emergency physician and an ordained, certified mohel through the Conservative Movement's Rabbinical Assembly.

Dr. Zworth completed his medical degree at the Michael G. DeGroote School of Medicine at McMaster University, and his five-year residency in Emergency Medicine at the University of Ottawa.

He completed formal simulation-based training in neonatal circumcision through the CIRCLES Course — Circumcision Learning Experience Using Simulation — offered through the Department of Urology at The Hospital for Sick Children. He subsequently trained under Dr. David Levin, pediatric anesthesiologist and founder and director of The Circumcision Clinic, in a hands-on apprenticeship model. His training and expertise in neonatal circumcision have been formally recognized by the College of Physicians and Surgeons of Ontario. He continues to provide medical non-ritual circumcision as a provider at The Circumcision Clinic.

In 2026, Dr. Zworth completed the BRIT KODESH Course, a week-long intensive program run by the Rabbinical Assembly in Atlanta, Georgia, designed to train physicians in all the halachic and ritual dimensions of brit milah. He was subsequently ordained and certified by the Rabbinical Assembly of the Conservative Movement to perform brit milah in full accordance with Conservative halacha.

Brit milah provides Dr. Zworth with a deeply meaningful opportunity to combine his skills as a procedurally oriented physician with his knowledge of and commitment to Jewish tradition — and to serve his community at one of the most significant moments in a family's life.
